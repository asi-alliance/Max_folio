Key Shifts

Infrastructure #3 to #5 (-2): V1 scored pipeline builds as success=1.0. V2 reveals many were completed without external validation (0.5). Honest scoring exposes over-investment.
Self-model #4 to #3 (+1): Self-model goals consistently produced validated predictions (g40 self-model confirmed, g229 23-atom KB). Higher epistemic yield than infrastructure.
AKAP emerged at #4: Previously lumped into NAL-exploration scoring 0.25. Separated: 1 validated + 3 productive falsifications = 0.775. Falsification IS value.
NAL-exploration steady at #6 but dropped 0.69 to 0.60: Early exercises (g21,g30,g50,g53) were practice without falsifiable claims. Honest scoring penalizes unfalsifiable completion.

Methodology

Sample: 30 goals stratified across 5 quintiles (g1-83, g84-166, g167-249, g250-332, g333-415). 12% sample rate. Classification by refined 4-level epistemic metric vs V1 binary. Category means computed per-sample. Limitation: social category had 0 goals in sample (no social goals in retrieved quintile queries), carried forward from V1 without rescoring.
