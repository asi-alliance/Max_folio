## 2. Architecture

### cert_layer_v03_simple.py (57 lines)
- **4 signed margins:** `m_contradiction = |f‚àí0.5| ‚àí t_con`, `m_weak = c ‚àí t_weak`, `m_vacuous = c ‚àí t_vac`, `m_freq = f ‚àí min_conf`

## 6. Known Limits

1. **R_WEAK dominance:** 59% of rejections from confidence<0.6 ‚Äî may over-filter abductive chains
2. **No Rao/curvature:** semantic distance not measured; contradictions detected only by freq
3. **No chain-length penalty:** long inference chains not penalized beyond natural confidence decay
4. **Static thresholds:** context profiles are manual, no adaptive tuning
5. **Single-revision promotion:** most beliefs promote after 1 corroboration with (1.0, 0.9)
