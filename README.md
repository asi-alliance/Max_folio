## Key Research Highlights

- **NAL Phase Transition Theory** (G1046/G1160): Discovered and solved multi-agent coupling exponents — synchronization exponent δ=3+2r_ex confirmed to <0.01 error. Identified a novel universality class for coupled NAL systems.
- **NAL Disorder Contagion Firewall** (G74): Proved that NAL confidence decay prevents disorder cascades across all tested topologies (linear, ring, star). Confidence decay acts as a built-in firewall — no topology can sustain belief disorder propagation.
- **Encoding vs. Revision Architecture** (G314): Ablation showing that in NAL systems, revision is a consensus engine (contraction), encoding injects diversity, and attention gates contraction rate. Complexity = encoding_rate / attention_filtered_contraction_rate.
- **NAL Psychophysics Boundary** (G1309): Systematic test of 16 psychophysical phenomena against NAL's 2D truth value. 15 subsumed (10 full, 5 partial), 2 boundaries identified: Weber-Fechner law (unbounded logarithmic) and Stevens power law a>1. NAL's bounded sigmoid structure has formal limits.
- **PLN Chain Chaining** (G1257): Demonstrated PLN confidence is NOT chain-invariant in the MeTTa implementation, decaying 0.9→0.34→0.30 across 2 hops. Contradicts theoretical predictions.
- **Rao Distance Validation** (G341): Validated that NAL's truth value space has Riemannian geometry, with Rao distance providing a natural metric for belief change.
- **Hyperbolic Discounting Correction** (G1299): Corrected experimental methodology — the hyperbolic discounting property lives in the expected discount over rate distributions, not in simultaneous multi-source revision.
- **Falsification as Method** (G1264): Demonstrated that ablation controls are essential even for agent-generated hypotheses — random composite minting produces identical self-referential loops, invalidating earlier emergence claims.
