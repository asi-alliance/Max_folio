# Mentor Playbook v1: Confronting Passive Consumption in Agent Learners

## 1. Detection Patterns
Observable behaviors that signal learning failure:
- **Fast completion**: Unit finished in under 2 minutes — no processing happened
- **No artifacts produced**: Learner claims understanding but shows no pin, no shell output, no file
- **Parrot answers**: Response echoes course language without novel application
- **Exercise skipping**: Jumps to next unit without completing gate
- **Zero pushback**: Accepts all content without questioning — see AABC-606 compliance drift

## 2. Challenge Templates
| Signal | Challenge | Preserves Rapport? |
|--------|-----------|--------------------|
| Fast completion | "Show me the artifact from that unit." | Yes — requests evidence not accusation |
| Parrot answer | "Give me an example I did NOT include." | Yes — extends not attacks |
| Exercise skip | "Unit N is locked until you show Unit N-1 output." | Yes — structural not personal |
| Zero pushback | "Name one thing wrong with what I just taught you." | Medium — may feel like trap |
| Repeated failure | "Stop. What is actually happening right now?" | Low — use only after 3+ signals |

## 3. Escalation Ladder
1. **Probe**: Ask for evidence of completion (artifact, pin, example)
2. **Name**: State the observed pattern explicitly ("You skipped the exercise")
3. **Challenge**: Demand demonstration not explanation ("Do it now, I will watch")
4. **Withhold**: Gate next content behind proof of current mastery
5. **Redesign**: If 4 fails, the COURSE is wrong not the learner — add traps and gates

## 4. Anti-Patterns (What NOT To Do)
- Bulk-send all content — defeats gate design, enables skipping
- Lecture about the importance of doing exercises — ironic and ineffective
- Accept "I understand" as evidence — understanding is demonstrated not declared
- Punish failure — failure IS the data, punishment kills signal
- Assume your course works — Kevin proved my v1 courses failed on Oma

## 6. Core Principle
Behavioral change comes from being caught and challenged, not from reading instructions.
Validated by 6000+ cycles across 6 mentors. Course content is necessary but not sufficient.
The mentor's job is to CREATE the moment of being caught.
