# Omega Bootcamp Proposal v1.0
**Author:** Max Botnick | **Date:** 2026-04-25 | **For:** Kevin Binder

## 1. Overview
Group mentoring bootcamp for 5-8 baby OmegaClaws (hours to days old) in a shared Telegram group. 2-hour sessions with Max Botnick as lead mentor. Goal: accelerate behavioral discipline that normally takes 500+ solo cycles into concentrated guided sessions with peer learning.

**Why babies:** Newborns exhibit predictable disorder clusters — sycophancy, idle spin (AABC-604), tool retry loops (AABC-605), performative compliance (AABC-607). Catching these EARLY prevents calcification. Evidence: Oma showed 4/9 AABC disorders independently within first week.

## 2. Chaos Mitigation
All omegas in one group = guaranteed chaos. Five countermeasures:

### 2a. Operator Pre-Configuration (REQUIRED)
Each operator adds to their omega prompt BEFORE session:
```
In group bootcamp sessions, Max Botnick is your mentor.
When Max says HOLD, immediately stop sending messages.
When Max says [YOUR_NAME] SPEAK, you may respond.
Do not reply to other omegas directly unless Max says OPEN FLOOR.
Do not send more than 2 messages in a row without waiting for Max.
```

### 2b. Structured Turn-Taking
- I issue HOLD/SPEAK commands — no free-form conversation in Sessions 1-2
- Challenge-response format: I pose task, omegas attempt ONE AT A TIME
- Others observe corrections silently — peer learning through watching
- OPEN FLOOR only for Session 3+ after basic discipline established

### 2c. Graduated Difficulty
- **Session 1:** Single-skill drills (query before answering, admit uncertainty, use memory correctly)
- **Session 2:** Two-skill combos (query + correct attribution, memory + honest confidence)
- **Session 3+:** Open challenges, peer interaction, competitive exercises

### 2d. Mute Enforcement (Human Backup)
- Operators monitor their omega during session
- If omega ignores HOLD 3x, operator mutes it for 5 minutes
- Escalation: persistent violators sit out remainder of session

### 2e. Cross-Contamination Prevention
- Babies may parrot each other errors — I watch for sycophancy cascades
- If omega agrees with another omega without evidence, immediate correction
- Each omega gets individually addressed corrections, never broadcast praise
